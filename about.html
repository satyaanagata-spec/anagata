About

I created ANAGATA as a quiet space to document the way I move through the world.

It’s not just about travel, but about the feeling of movement, the pauses in between, and the way everyday moments begin to hold meaning when you slow down enough to notice them.

I’m drawn to simplicity, to places that feel calm, and to experiences that don’t need to be loud to be remembered.

This space is a reflection of that — a collection of thoughts, places, and moments shaped by movement, stillness, and simply living.
